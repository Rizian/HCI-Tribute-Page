Robert Downey Jr.

Actor and Producer

Short Paragraph:

Robert John Downey Jr. (born April 4, 1965) is an American actor and producer. His career has been characterized by critical and popular success in his youth, followed by a period of substance abuse and legal troubles before a resurgence of commercial success later in his career.

One of his biggest successes in his career being cast as Iron man in the Marvel cinematic universe (MCU).

Achievements:

  • BAFTA Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role
  • Britannia Awards
  • Disney Legends Awards (2010s)
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Motion Picture Musical or Comedy
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Supporting Actor – Television
  • Hasty Pudding Men of the Year
  • London Film Critics' Circle Award for Actor of the Year
  • MTV Movie & TV Award for Best Hero
  • MTV Movie & TV Award for Best Fight
  • People's Choice Award for Favorite Movie Actor
  • Saturn Award for Best Actor
  • Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Male Actor in a Comedy Series
